Questions

Before you begin

How long does the assessment take?
Usually 5–7 minutes. There are 36 gentle questions, asked one at a time, with no right or wrong answers.
Is this therapy?
No. The Clarity Method is a wellness and self-reflection tool. It is not medical or psychological treatment, does not diagnose any condition, and is not a substitute for care from a qualified professional.
What do I get for free?
Your full Clarity Map — six inner-life scores out of 100 — plus a personal emotional portrait, and a free 7-day personalized healing practice. No credit card needed.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your answers are stored securely, never sold, and used only to personalize your practice.
What happens after the assessment?
You see your results instantly on this page. Then, if you choose, you begin a free 7-day practice in the app — journaling, guided meditations, and sound frequencies built around your focus area. Ongoing plans start from ₹499 per month after the trial.
Do I need to download anything?
No. The assessment and your results happen right here in your browser.
